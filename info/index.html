Info: Source Hunter

Introduction

Welcome to Source Hunter! This is the place where curious minds come together to solve the mystery behind the origins of the images and memes we see online. Powered by blockchain technology, Source Hunter is the perfect fusion of community-driven research and rewarding contributions with BTT tokens.

How It Works

Here’s how you can start your journey with Source Hunter:

  • Submit an Image: Upload an image link, ask your question, and wait for the community to help!
  • Get Community Help: Other users will comment and try to find the source for your image.
  • Vote & Reward: Upvote helpful comments, and send BTT tokens to reward great research.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What is Source Hunter? Source Hunter is a platform where users ask for help finding the origin of images, and the community helps through research and rewards.
  • How do I submit an image? Simply paste the image link, add your question, and submit!
  • How do I earn BTT tokens? Contribute by commenting on posts, and if your answer helps, you may receive BTT tokens as a reward.

Why Blockchain?

At Source Hunter, we chose the BitTorrent Chain (BTTC) because it gives us exactly what we need: fast transactions, low fees, and seamless integration. With BTTC, users can send BTT tokens quickly and without the headache of high transaction costs, making it perfect for a platform where community contributions are frequently rewarded. The scalability of BTTC ensures that whether it's one or a thousand users interacting, the experience remains smooth. So every time you appreciate someone’s help by sending tokens, BTTC makes sure it happens instantly and efficiently.

Our Future Vision

We have big plans for Source Hunter! In the future, we aim to:

  • Expand the platform to cover a broader range of media types, including audio and video sources.
  • Introduce advanced image recognition tools to make the source discovery process even faster and more accurate.
  • Enhance the user experience with more detailed contributor profiles and achievements.
